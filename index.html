DeFridge

The Sustainable Way to Declutter

Easily manage buying healthy, ethically sourced food and reducing food waste in a sustainable manner! DeFridge offers users a convenient way to organize meal shopping, prevent food waste and participate in mutual aid by donating excessive groceries to those in need.



"An intuitive solution to an intersectional problem-- mutual aid wrapped up in a single app!"

Scan

Use CamScanner to efficiently scan your newly brought groceries as you bring them into your home! This will automatically save details such as expiration date and nutrition facts to your Fridge itinerary.

Wallet

Link your credit cards, Apple wallet, or food stamp apps (i.e. Fresh EBT) directly to your iFridge wallet to keep track of your grocery spending in one place.

Fridge

Keep your fridge itinerary in one place! Based on your this, DeFridge will remind you to utilize ingredients before they expire.

Shop

Before your groceries experience even begins, our app provides you with nearby stores, brands and coupons.

Donate

DeFridge connects you with local non-profits to share your ingredients with-- promoting mutual aid through a sustainable fridge clean-out.

Cook

Our AI algorithms will use your fridge itinerary to customize and suggest new, exciting recipes for your meal rotation!



Reminders

Based on your fridge itinerary, DeFridge will remind you to utilize ingredients before they expire.

A Growing Health Crisis in the US.

What we're trying to change.


What is Food Insecurity?

  • Lack of adequate access or ability to provide proper nutrition due to financial struggles, health problems, other impeding issues
  • 10.5% of all households consume poor and limited diets
  • Contributes to many chronic diseases such as heart disease, hypertension, diabetes

Food Insecurity Disporportionatality

  • POC are affected by a disproportionately higher risk of hunger
  • In 2018, 22.5% of black homes and 18.5% of Latino/Hispanic households experienced food insecurity

US Food Waste

  • Each year, 108 billion pounds of food is wasted in the United States
  • AKA 130 billion meals and more than $408 billion in food thrown away each year
  • Nearly 40% of all food in America is wasted


How we're helping

  • Connecting ebt, debit, credit to the in-app wallet
  • Keeping you accountable based on what you have at home
  • Have food that's about to expire? Find local non-profits to donate to or use our amazing recipes!
  • Combining daily life/nutrition AND social justice all in one app by offering a tangible way to reduce waste
  • Raising awareness surrounding food insecurity
